Join us at Tech Pragati 2024

Tech Pragati 2024 emerges as a vibrant nexus where students, tech enthusiasts, industry leaders, and professionals converge to celebrate innovation, technology, and entrepreneurship. This groundbreaking event offers an immersive experience featuring engaging seminars, workshops, tech showcases, and invaluable networking opportunities. At the heart of Tech Pragati lies a dynamic array of challenges, including a stimulating hackathon, a fiercely competitive coding competition, thrilling robotics challenges, and an inspiring Ideathon. These hands-on competitions serve as the pinnacle of collaborative innovation, encouraging participants to push boundaries, solve real-world problems, and showcase their technological prowess.

Join us at Tech Pragati as we explore the latest technological advancements, connect with industry experts, and foster a collective passion for innovation.

Skill Contests

Tue, Feb 12th 2024
Welcome to the Skill Fusion Challenge, where creativity meets mastery in fields like photography and videography! Shutterbugs and filmmakers, showcase your skills in a thrilling contest where artistic brilliance and technical expertise collide. Participate, share your visionary work, and let your talent shine. Engage in discussions with fellow artists, explore the depths of your craft, and let the Skill Fusion Challenge be the stage where your creativity takes center stage!

Hackathon

Tue, Feb 12th 2024
Gear up for a sustainable development challenge at our Hackathon! Join forces with fellow innovators, code wizards, and problem solvers to tackle real-world issues. Embrace the adrenaline rush as you collaborate, ideate, and create groundbreaking solutions within a limited timeframe. Unleash your creativity and technical prowess for a chance to shine!

Coding Competition

Tue, Feb 12th 2024
Calling all coding enthusiasts! Dive into a thrilling Coding Competition where precision, speed, and ingenuity reign supreme. Test your coding finesse against the clock, solve mind-bending problems, and showcase your programming skills. Get ready to write the next chapter of coding excellence and emerge victorious!

Robotics

Tue, Feb 12th 2024
Get ready to bring your bots to life! The Robotics Challenges await, where innovation meets technology. Engage in thrilling competitions designed to push the boundaries of robotics. Design, build, and maneuver your creations through challenging tasks. Join us for a high-octane display of engineering brilliance!

Ideathon

Tue, Feb 12th 2024
Engage in an Ideathon that encourages a fusion of business and technology. Here, aspiring entrepreneurs and innovators pitch business ideas intertwined with tech ecosystems. Dive into discussions, pitch your visionary ideas, and explore the intersection of technology, innovation, and entrepreneurial spirit.
